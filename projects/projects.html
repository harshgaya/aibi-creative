Project-1

Laundry Service

Overview

Mobile App and Dashboard
Website For Laundry Service

We created a mobile application that revolutionized the laundry industry,
enabling users to schedule, track, and pay for laundry services seamlessly.
With a focus on user convenience and automation, the app provides
an end-to-end solution for laundry businesses.

Client Objective

The client sought to develop a user-friendly platform
that could handle booking management, payment
integration, and real-time tracking. The goal was
to provide customers with a convenient solution for
their laundry needs while streamlining business operations.

Solution

We designed the app with Flutter, making it compatible
with both Android and iOS. The React.js dashboard provided the client with
full control over the backend, while MongoDB allowed us to manage
a scalable and secure database. We also integrated real-time
notifications and a robust payment gateway for a seamless user
experience.

Technology Used

1. Mobile App: Flutter
2. Admin Dashboard: React.js
3. API: Node.js
4. Database: MongoDB
5. Cloud: AWS

Features

1. User Registration & Authentication: Secure login system.
2. Real-time Order Tracking: Users can monitor their orders in real-time.
3. Payment Gateway: Integration with Stripe and PayPal.
4. Push Notifications: Alerts for order status updates.
5. Admin Panel: Full control over orders and users via a React.js dashboard.

Outcome

The app was a success, with over 10,000 downloads within the first
three months. It gained registration from 150 colleges, serving
over 10,000 students, and generating a monthly revenue of 5 lakh.
User engagement increased by 40%, and laundry business owners
reported improved efficiency and customer satisfaction.
